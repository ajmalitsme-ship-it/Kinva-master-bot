Getting Started

How do I create an account?

You can create a free account in three ways:

  • Click "Sign Up" on the top right corner
  • Use your Google account to sign in
  • Connect with Telegram for instant access

No credit card required for the free plan!

What's included in the free plan?

The free plan includes:

  • Basic video editing (up to 60 seconds)
  • 10 image filters
  • 100+ design templates
  • 720p export quality
  • 3 exports per day
  • 100MB cloud storage

Upgrade to premium for unlimited access!

How do I start my first project?

Starting a project is easy:

  1. Click on "Design Editor", "Video Editor", or "Image Editor"
  2. Choose a template or start from scratch
  3. Upload your media files
  4. Use the tools to edit and enhance
  5. Export and download your creation

Check our video tutorials for step-by-step guides!

Video Editing

What video formats are supported?

We support all major video formats:

  • MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV
  • WEBM, FLV, M4V
  • 3GP, MPEG

Maximum file size: 500MB for free users, 2GB for premium

How do I add effects to my video?

Adding effects is simple:

  1. Upload your video to the editor
  2. Click on the "Effects" tab in the right panel
  3. Browse through 20+ effects
  4. Click on an effect to apply it
  5. Adjust intensity with the slider

Premium users get access to exclusive effects like Neon, Glitch, and 3D!

Can I add transitions between clips?

Yes! You can add transitions between multiple clips:

  1. Add multiple clips to the timeline
  2. Select the transition type from the transitions tab
  3. Drag and drop between clips
  4. Adjust transition duration

Available transitions: Fade, Slide, Wipe, Zoom, Rotate, and more!

How do I change video speed?

To change video speed:

  1. Select the clip in the timeline
  2. Open the properties panel
  3. Adjust the speed slider (0.25x to 4x)
  4. Preview the result

Perfect for slow motion or time-lapse effects!

Image Editing

What image formats are supported?

We support all common image formats:

  • JPG, JPEG, PNG
  • GIF, WebP, BMP
  • TIFF, ICO

Maximum file size: 50MB

How do I remove background from an image?

Background removal is available for premium users:

  1. Upload your image to the editor
  2. Click on "Background Removal" in the tools panel
  3. AI will automatically detect and remove background
  4. Fine-tune with the eraser tool if needed

Perfect for product photos and portraits!

How many filters are available?

We offer 30+ professional filters:

  • Color filters: Vintage, Cinematic, Sepia, Black & White
  • Artistic filters: Oil Paint, Watercolor, Sketch
  • Effect filters: Glitch, Neon, Pixelate
  • Adjustments: Brightness, Contrast, Saturation, Temperature

Premium users get access to exclusive filters!

Can I use templates?

Yes! We have 1000+ ready-to-use templates:

  • Social Media: Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, YouTube
  • Business: Presentations, Business Cards, Flyers
  • Education: Worksheets, Posters, Infographics
  • Events: Invitations, Banners, Announcements

Browse templates from the templates gallery!

How do layers work?

Layers help you organize your design elements:

  • Each element (text, image, shape) is on its own layer
  • Reorder layers by dragging up or down
  • Hide, lock, or duplicate layers
  • Adjust layer opacity and blend modes

Use layers to create complex designs easily!

How do I upgrade to premium?

Upgrading is simple:

  1. Click on "Premium" in the navigation menu
  2. Choose your plan (Monthly, Yearly, or Lifetime)
  3. Select payment method (Card, PayPal, UPI, Crypto)
  4. Complete the payment
  5. Premium features activate instantly!

We offer a 7-day free trial for new users!

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime:

  1. Go to Settings → Billing
  2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
  3. Confirm cancellation
  4. You'll keep premium until the end of the billing period

No cancellation fees or hidden charges!

Account & Billing

How do I change my password?

To change your password:

  1. Go to Settings → Account
  2. Enter your current password
  3. Enter your new password
  4. Click "Change Password"

Make sure your new password is strong and unique!

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:

  1. Go to Settings → Account
  2. Scroll to "Danger Zone"
  3. Click "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm with your password

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone!

How do I view my invoices?

To view your invoices:

  1. Go to Settings → Billing
  2. Scroll to "Invoice History"
  3. Click "Download PDF" next to any invoice

All invoices are available for download!
