Our Story

Kinva Master was founded in 2024 with a simple vision: to create a platform that combines the best of Canva and Kinemaster in one place. We noticed that creators were switching between multiple tools to edit videos, images, and designs. We wanted to change that.

Today, Kinva Master is used by over 500,000 creators, from hobbyists to professionals, who trust our platform to bring their ideas to life. Our team is committed to continuous innovation and delivering the best creative experience possible.
